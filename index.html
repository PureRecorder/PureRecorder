About PureRecorder

PureRecorder is a high-efficiency background engine designed for seamless, 24/7 Chaturbate stream archiving. It eliminates the need for manual monitoring, providing a "set-and-forget" environment that captures your favorite content the moment it goes live. Built for stability and precision, the software handles the entire lifecycle of a broadcast—from detection to final storage—without requiring user intervention.

Automated Vigilance

The core engine polls your target list every 30 seconds, ensuring that recording starts the millisecond a stream is detected.

High-Fidelity Preservation

Full support for resolutions up to 1440p, ensuring your archives maintain the original broadcast quality.

Lossless Archiving (PRO)

Our advanced capture method creates a bit-for-bit copy of the stream data directly to your disk, resulting in zero quality loss.

System Integrity

Integrated health checks monitor your network and database status in real-time, ensuring the software is always primed and ready.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PureRecorder and our licensing.

What is PureRecorder?
PureRecorder is an automated recording management tool designed to monitor and capture Chaturbate live streams. It handles multiple performers simultaneously, ensuring you never miss a broadcast.
What is the difference between the Free and PRO versions?
The Free version allows for basic monitoring and recording, while the PRO version unlocks advanced features like higher resolution/FPS settings, priority support, and unlimited performer slots.
Is my data private?
Yes. PureRecorder runs locally on your machine. Your database, configuration, and recordings are stored in your local directory and are not uploaded to any servers.
Can I use my license on multiple computers?
Licenses are tied to a single Machine ID to prevent abuse. If you need to migrate your license to a new PC, please contact support.
What happens when my license expires?
The application will revert to the "Free" version functionality. Your data and performers will remain safe, but PRO features will be locked until renewal.
The performer is live, but PureRecorder isn't recording. Why?
This usually indicates that the software is being blocked by the platform's security or age-gate. Cookies are mandatory for the system to function correctly.
